Benefits of Named ACL over Numbered ACL method:
==========================================
1. In named ACL, no need to remember acl range, we can directly specify type(standard/extended).
2. You cannot delete a specific ACL statement in numbered ACL, if do so, will delete entire ACL rules configured, but it is possible to delete in named ACL.

3. You can specify name for ACL statements, that makes easier to understand the use of the written statement.
