Ethernet cable **colors do NOT affect performance, speed, or bandwidth**.  
Colors are used **only for organization and labeling**, mainly in IT environments.

---

## âœ… Do Different Ethernet Cable Colors Represent Different Speeds?

**No.**  
Cable color has **no technical meaning** and **does not impact performance**.

IT teams choose colors for easier cable management, such as:

- Identifying different networks (e.g., Production vs Testing)
- Separating voice/data/VLANs
- Reducing confusion in racks and patch panels

### Common (non-standard) color conventions:

| Color | Typical Usage (Custom Practice) |
|-------|---------------------------------|
| **Blue** | General data connections |
| **Yellow** | PoE (Power over Ethernet) |
| **Red** | Secure networks / DMZ |
| **Green** | Management interfaces |
